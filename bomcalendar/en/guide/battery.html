Excluding Bom Calendar from battery optimization will not have a significant impact on your battery efficiency since Bom Calendar uses a minimal amount of memory in the background for alerts only.

  1. How to Optimize Battery Usage: Tap 'Go To Android Settings' at the bottom of the screen and then tap 'Manage Battery Usage.'

    Select 'App Not Optimized' at the top of the screen, then click 'Select All' from the menu.

    Find Bom Calendar on the list of apps and set the switch to OFF.
